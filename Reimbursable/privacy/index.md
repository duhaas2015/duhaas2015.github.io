# Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 1, 2026

Thank you for using our app ("App"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices. This policy is designed to help you complete App Store Connect’s App Privacy details and to inform users transparently.

If you have questions, contact us at: duhaas+appstore@gmail.com

## Summary
- We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide and improve the App.
- We do not sell your data.
- We do not use your data for tracking across other companies’ apps or websites.
- Data is retained only as long as needed for the stated purposes.

## Data Collection and Use
Depending on your configuration and usage, the App may collect the following categories of data. You can customize this section to match your actual practices for App Store Connect.

### Contact Info
- Data: Email address or other contact details you voluntarily provide (e.g., when contacting support).
- Purpose: Customer support and communication.
- Linked to you: Yes, when you provide it directly.
- Tracking: No.

### Identifiers
- Data: Device identifiers (e.g., a generated app-specific identifier) if needed for core functionality, analytics, or diagnostics.
- Purpose: App functionality, security, or usage analytics.
- Linked to you: Potentially, if identifiers are stored with account or device info.
- Tracking: No cross-app tracking.

### Usage Data
- Data: Interactions, in-app events, and performance metrics.
- Purpose: Improve app stability, features, and user experience; diagnose issues.
- Linked to you: Aggregated or de-identified where possible.
- Tracking: No.

### Diagnostics
- Data: Crash logs and performance data.
- Purpose: Identify and fix bugs; improve reliability.
- Linked to you: Aggregated or de-identified where possible.
- Tracking: No.

### Location (Optional)
- Data: Approximate or precise location, only if a feature requires it (e.g., location-based content).
- Purpose: Provide the feature you requested.
- Linked to you: Only while using the feature.
- Tracking: No.

## Tracking
We do not use data to track you across other companies’ apps and websites. If we introduce advertising or cross-app measurement in the future, we will request App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission and update this policy.

## Your Choices and Rights
- Access and Correction: You can request a copy of your data and ask us to correct inaccuracies.
- Deletion: You can request deletion of your data, subject to legal obligations.
- Opt-Out: You can disable analytics or crash reporting where supported in the App’s settings.
- Permissions: You can manage system permissions (e.g., location) in iOS Settings.

To make a request, contact: support@example.com. We may need to verify your identity.

## Security
